工资类型Pay Types

工资种类 Two Types of Pay

您是 "时薪" 还是 "年薪"？ — 两者很不一样 Hourly vs Salary — they work very differently
时薪 HourlyHourly Pay
按小时计算工资Paid by the hour
  • 每小时多少钱$X per hour
  • 超过 40 小时算加班 (1.5×)Over 40 hrs/week = overtime (1.5×)
  • 必须打卡 — 记录工时Must clock in/out — track all hours
  • 病假、假期可能少Fewer benefits in some jobs
年薪 SalarySalary Pay
固定金额，无论工作几小时Fixed amount regardless of hours
  • 每年总数 (例：$50,000)Total per year (e.g. $50,000)
  • 通常没有加班费Usually no overtime pay
  • 福利通常较好Typically better benefits
  • 需有学历或专业经验Usually requires degree or expertise
💡 发薪频率 Pay Frequency 💡 Pay Frequency
"Biweekly" 双周发薪 = 每两周一次 (一年26次) · "Semi-monthly" 月内两次 = 每月15号和月底 (一年24次) "Biweekly" = every 2 weeks (26/year) · "Semi-monthly" = twice a month, often 15th and last day (24/year)

W-4 表格W-4 Form

填写 W-4 表 Filling Out the W-4

什么是 W-4？告诉雇主从工资里扣多少税 The W-4 tells your employer how much tax to withhold
  1. 填写基本信息Step 1 — Personal Info
    Step 1 — 姓名、地址、社安号 (SSN) 或 ITIN、申报身份 (单身/已婚)Name, address, SSN or ITIN, filing status (single / married)
  2. 多份工作？Step 2 — Multiple Jobs?
    Step 2 — 如果有两份工，或配偶也工作 — 勾这里Check this box if you have 2+ jobs or your spouse also works
  3. 家属减免Step 3 — Dependents
    Step 3 — 17岁以下小孩 × $2,000 · 其他家属 × $500Children under 17: $2,000 each · Other dependents: $500 each
  4. 其他调整 (可跳过)Step 4 — Optional Adjustments
    Step 4 — 其他收入、想多扣的税 — 大多数人留空Other income or extra withholding — most people leave blank
  5. 签字、写日期Step 5 — Sign & Date
    Step 5 — 签名确认 — 必须签字才生效Sign and date — required to take effect
💡 拿不准？先按 "单身、0 家属" 填 — 多扣一点，报税时会退回。 💡 Not sure? File "Single / 0 dependents" — extra withholding gets refunded at tax time.
华埠服务中心可以帮您填 — 一对一辅导，免费。 CSC can help you fill it out — free one-on-one help.

谈判与接受Negotiate or Accept?

什么时候可以谈？ When to Negotiate vs Accept

谈判不是讨价还价 — 是替自己说话。雇主预期您会问。 Negotiating isn't haggling — it's speaking up for yourself. Employers expect it.
✓ 可以直接接受✓ Accept the Offer
  • 薪水与您调研的相符Pay matches your research
  • 福利好 (医保、401(k)、PTO)Strong benefits package
  • 是您唯一的 Offer，急需工作It's your only offer and you need work
  • 小时工/入门职位 — 通常不谈Hourly entry-level — usually not negotiable
→ 可以谈一谈→ Try to Negotiate
  • 您有同行业经验或技能You have relevant experience or skills
  • 薪水低于市场行情Offer is below market rate for the role
  • 您有另一份 Offer 比较You have another competing offer
  • 薪水不能谈 — 可谈入职奖金、PTO、远程If pay is fixed, ask about bonus, PTO, remote
💬 简单话术 — 礼貌专业 💬 Simple Script — Polite & Professional
"Thank you for the offer. Based on my experience, would you consider $X?" — 谢谢您的录用。考虑到我的经验，可以给到 $X 吗？ 在美国，问一句不会得罪人 — 这是替自己说话，不是不识抬举。 "Thank you for the offer. Based on my experience, would you consider $X?" — Asking once is normal in the U.S. It's advocating for yourself, not being ungrateful.
